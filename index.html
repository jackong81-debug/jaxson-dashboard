Safety Briefing
All staff must be aware of these safety requirements before the event.
🔥
Firepit — Never Leave Unattended
At least one staff member must be present at the firepit at all times when lit. Monitor children near fire constantly. Jack to designate responsible staff member.
💧
Lake Area — Children Supervised At All Times
No child is to be near the lake without adult/staff supervision. When fishing activity is running, at least one staff member must be stationed at the lake.
🧒
Children Near Lodge Exits
During treasure hunt and evening activities, monitor all lodge exits. Ensure no child wanders toward the lake or road unsupervised.
✅ Pre-Event Safety Checklist
Firepit area checked and safe for use
Lake boundary checked — children's access points noted
First aid kit located and accessible
Emergency exits identified in lodge
All staff briefed on firepit supervision roster
Lake supervision roster confirmed with Jack
Dietary requirements communicated to kitchen
Fishing rods stored safely until Jack sets up
Bathrooms stocked with emergency supplies
Final packdown — firepit fully extinguished
